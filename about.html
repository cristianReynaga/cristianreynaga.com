    Visual artist and interaction designer. He has been working exploring and connecting mostly physical interfaces and electronic devices with people, not only users. Interested in the way people interact with their technological environment as a medium to interact with other humans.

    Also interested in working with groups and collaborating with colleagues, he runs the Processing Buenos Aires Meetup and Internet of Things Buenos Aires Meetup with friends and artists from Argentina.

    Formed as a musician, worked as a 3D artist, later became an interactivity programmer and is a grateful user of Processing, openFrameworks and Arduino.

    Lives in Buenos Aires, Argentina.
